Cities

The population density of our cities has a significant influence on quality of life. Population density is simply a measure of the number of people per unit area. There are significant costs and significant benefits for each style of constructing a city. A city with low population density, enables residents to have yards. But it also means people need to drive places because amenities are often further away. Higher population density generally results in a greater selection of restaurants, bars, cafes, and cultural amenities closer to someone's house. But even within cities, population density is generally extremely varied. This map allows you to explore the population density of New York City.
